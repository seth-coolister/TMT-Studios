About Us
Hi, I'm Tayler!
I am an independent game developer looking to bring small games to BIG audiences! I've love rogue-like games in all their forms. Whether it's an action like Binding of Isaac or strategy like Slay The Spire you'll always find me going for that perfect run. I've also been influenced by metroidvanias such like Hollowknight (Silksong when???) and classic JRPGS like the Final Fantasy series. Currently I am working on more rogue-like goodness and can't wait to create your next hidden gem!
